6. Conclusion
6.1 Interrupting the libidinal economy - cyber rebel, Gorri칩n
The cyborgs or cyborg-ciphers in Argentine culture are clearly condensing anxieties surrounding the loss of social identities and cultural memory, and the reinscription of these disembedded identities within global regimes governing the circulation of goods, information and, crucially, images.
Moebius deploys a paradoxical topology at the heart of the cybercity – that of the Buenos Aires underground transformed into a Möbius strip – in order to provide a spatial figure of memory, or rather, to provide a method of recovering memory and temporality from the very heart of the deterritorialized spatial labyrinths of the megalopolis.
La sonámbula on the other hand, deploys its cyborgs at the threshold of the failure of representation, on the spatio-temporal boundary where representational logic phases into and out of an order of simulation. The technologically mediated dreams of an Argentine Ève future, are self-consciously bound up with a collective dream of liberation and escape, as we see in this last clip where rebel Gorrión interrupts a television advertisement, although no-one is watching.
