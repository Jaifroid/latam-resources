2. Argentine tradition
2.1 Engagement with cinema as mechanical simulation of absent bodies
There is a long tradition in Argentina of engagement with cinema and other forms of technology as the mechanical simulation of absent bodies.
2.1.1 Disavowal of the disappeared body behind the screen
I haven't got time to go into it here, but in the aftermath of dictatorship a number of films activate the idea of disavowal in cinema (and splitting of belief) as parallel to the disavowal of the bodies of the disappeared within Argentine society during the dictatorship.
